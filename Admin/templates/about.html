About the Solar Panel Washer

This system is designed to ensure your solar panels stay clean, enhancing their efficiency and lifespan. It supports both Automatic and Manual modes to meet your convenience and safety requirements.

  • Auto Mode: Operates automatically based on a predefined schedule (e.g., every 7 or 15 days). This ensures your panels remain clean without any manual intervention.
  • Manual Mode: Allows you to start a wash manually. However, this mode is only available between 7 PM and 7 AM to prevent thermal shock risks.
Why Manual Mode is Time-Restricted:
  • Washing during the day can crack or damage solar panels due to heat from sunlight.
  • Cooler nighttime temperatures reduce thermal stress, making it safer to clean.
  • Protects panel coating and improves overall durability.

By using this system, you help your solar installation work at peak performance while extending its operational life. Stay green and efficient!
